About AsiaPay Indonesia

PT AsiaPay Technology Indonesia, or simply AsiaPay Indonesia, is a wholly owned subsidiary of AsiaPay Limited which is one of the leading payment service providers in the Asia Pacific region. We deliver secure and reliable payment services for businesses of all sizes, helping them to reduce fraud, lower costs and improve selling efficiency in both local and international markets.

Founded in 2000, AsiaPay is a premier digital payment solution and technology vendor, strive to bring advanced, secured, integrated, and cost-effective digital payment processing solutions and services to banks and eBusinesses around the world. Our services are in abundance, covering international credit and debit cards, bank account / net-banking, digital wallets, prepaid cards, over-the-counters etc.

AsiaPay is an accredited payment processor and payment gateway solution vendor for banks, a certified IPSP for merchants and certified international 3-D Secure vendor for Visa, Mastercard, American Express, JCB, Discover Global Network and UnionPay.

AsiaPay offers a variety of award-winning, multi-currency, multi-lingual, multi-card, and multi-channeled payment solutions. Alongside with our advanced fraud detection and management solutions, we are an ideal payment provider.

Headquartered in Hong Kong, we offer professional digital payment solution consultancy and quality local service support across 15 operative offices in Asia-Pacific, including: Hong Kong, Australia (Melbourne and Sydney), China (Guangzhou, Shanghai and Shenzhen), India, Indonesia (Bali and Jakarta), Malaysia, Philippines, Singapore, Taiwan, Thailand, Vietnam and United Kingdom.

We developed the payment gateway PayDollar to provide secure and seamless digital payment solutions for both SME's and large enterprises in worldwide. Our online payment system was developed to provide local merchants in Indonesia to accept digital payments ranging from credit cards via Visa, Mastercard, JCB, American Express, ATM Transfer via MANDIRI , BCA , BRI ,BNI, DANAMON, PERMATA, internet banking via CIMB Click , BCA Klikpay and Epay BRI, over-the-counter payment via Indomaret and Alfamart, eWallet via OVO and DANA and alternative payment methods such as KREDIVO.
